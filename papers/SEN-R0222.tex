\noindent
ABSTRACT\smallskip

\noindent
  This paper studies coalgebras from the perspective of finite 
    observations.  
    We introduce the notion of finite step equivalence and a corresponding
    category with finite step equivalence-preserving morphisms.
    This category always has a final object, which generalises the canonical
    model construction from Kripke models to coalgebras. 
    We then turn to logics whose formulae are invariant under finite step
    equivalence, which we call logics of rank $\omega$. 
    For these logics, we use topological methods and give a characterisation
    of compact logics and definable classes of models.
