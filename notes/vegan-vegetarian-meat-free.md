The modern history of veganism has a definite turning point. It is known when and how the term was invented. The first [vegan newsletter](https://issuu.com/vegan_society/docs/the_vegan_news_1944) appeared in 1944. This [interview with Donald Watson](https://www.vegansociety.com/sites/default/files/DW_Interview_2002_Unabridged_Transcript.pdf) is a fascinating source on the history.

## Recepies

In January 2020, we took part in [Veganuary](https://us.veganuary.com/), a month of vegan food. We didn't take the pledge but ended up making very little exceptions. To our surprise, we ate not only healthier, but also had more delicious food on the table than ever before. Part of the reason, of course, was that we had to try many new recipies and variety and diversity always makes cooking more interesting.

Apart from the curiosity to seek out new recipies, we were also motivated by thinking about factory farming and climate change. Nothing moves parents as much as the questions asked by their children.

I collect here some of the recipies that we keep coming back to for inspiration.

In the meanwhile the coronavirus lockdown let us to grow our own vegetables in some raised beds. Tomatoes, zucchini, aubergines, mince, chili, peppers, ... but most importantly basil. Fresh basil is such an amazing ingredient.

## One Pot Dishes

These recipes can be varied in so many ways. Which pasta recipe cannot be prepared in a one-pot version?

- [Creamy Vegan Broccoli Pasta](https://delightfuladventures.com/wprm_print/8527)

- [One Pot Pasta](https://www.marthastewart.com/978784/one-pan-pasta):  We use 11 cups of water for 24 oz of pasta. (Or 6 cups for a pound or 16 oz pasta is easy to remember). I usually fry the onions, garlic, mushrooms, etc first. 

- [One Pot Mushroom Rice](https://damndelicious.net/2015/10/21/one-pot-mushroom-rice/)

- [One Pot Ratatouille Rice](https://yupitsvegan.com/one-pot-ratatouille-rice/#wprm-recipe-container-6463). Since we grow our own fresh veggies this is one of our favourite recipes.

- [Mushroom Stroganoff](https://www.simplyquinoa.com/vegan-mushroom-stroganoff/). Auch ohne Pilze und/oder mit Spinat. Besonders lecker mit Gnocchi.

- [Mushroom Stroganoff with Cashewnutcream](https://frommybowl.com/one-pot-mushroom-stroganoff/). Combines many ideas: Mushrooms instead of meat, cashewnut butter instead of cream, one pot pasta.
